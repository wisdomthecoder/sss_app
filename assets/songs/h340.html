1 To-day the Saviour calls :
Ye wanderers, come ;
Oh, ye benighted souls,
Why longer roam ?

2 To-day the Saviour calls :
Oh, listen now !
Within these sacred walls
To Jesus bow.

3 To-day the Saviour calls :
For refuge fly ;
The storm of justice falls,
And death is nigh.

4 The Spirit calls to-day :
Yield to His power ;
Oh, grieve Him not away !
is mercy's hour.
